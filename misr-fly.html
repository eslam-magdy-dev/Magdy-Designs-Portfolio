Book with Ease

Brief

Misr Fly is transforming the flight booking experience for Egyptian travelers, offering a seamless platform to explore both domestic and international destinations. Prioritizing convenience and comfort , Misr Fly’s design makes planning and booking flights an effortless experience.

Starting thoughts…

Many Egyptian travelers seek quick , reliable booking without needing additional apps. Misr Fly meets this need by providing a user-centered approach to booking, designed for intuitive and accessible use.

Solution

Flight Booking Simplified

Misr Fly’s interface makes booking flights easier . Whether it’s selecting your travel destination , choosing flexible dates , or completing a secure payment , the platform provides a streamlined experience for users. By focusing on user needs, Misr Fly ensures travelers can effortlessly plan their trips without hassle .

Simple Flight Booking

Booking flights shouldn’t be a complex process. Misr Fly ensures you have a smooth experience, from selecting destinations to choosing flight dates and making payments. With user-friendly navigation, booking your next adventure is just a few clicks away.

Discover Your Next Place

The homepage showcases top vacation destinations with visually engaging images, making it easier to select your dream vacation. Whether you're traveling to Cairo, Dubai, or Riyadh, the clear layout and concise information make the destination choice easy and exciting.

Flexible Date

With Misr Fly's flexible date feature, users can view available flight options at a glance. Easily switch between dates and compare prices, ensuring you find the best deal. The simple grid layout organizes flights by time, duration, and price, allowing users to make informed decisions without confusion.

Secure & Streamlined Payment

Misr Fly offers a seamless payment experience. The payment screen simplifies the process by displaying all booking details clearly, ensuring users can review and confirm their flight choices easily. Flexible payment options and a secure gateway provide peace of mind.

01.
DISCOVER

How can we enhance the flight booking experience for Egyptian travelers?

Secondary Research

Conducted thorough market research on Egyptian and regional travel industries. Analyzed user frustrations with booking processes and identified local traveler needs. Insights showed preference for simple pricing, flexible dates, and streamlined booking. Demand for transparent fees and reliable options was high.

User Interview

Conducted interviews with five travelers who regularly book flights online. Participants highlighted frustrations with hidden fees, limited flexible dates, and difficulties finding essential flight details. These insights were pivotal in identifying user-centered priorities for Misr Fly’s design.

Competitor Analysis

Evaluated both local and global competitors to identify gaps and strengths. EgyptAir and FlyEgypt were primary local competitors, both lacking intuitive digital experiences. Global competitors, like Qatar Airways, excelled in customer service but often at premium prices, creating an opportunity for Misr Fly to offer accessible, streamlined booking with localized features.

02.
EMPATHIZE

Understanding user pain points and goals

User Persona & Journey

Developed the persona, Yaseen El-Sayed, a frequent traveler who values a straightforward, clear booking process. Yaseen prioritizes convenience, transparent pricing, and streamlined steps for seat selection and payment. This persona guided our focus on a clean, user-friendly interface that reduces unnecessary steps and ensures clarity in every part of the booking flow.

03.
IDEATE

How can we make booking with Misr Fly easy, efficient, and adaptable?

Problem Statements

After analyzing Laila’s journey, I identified three main challenges:

  1. How might we provide a quick, intuitive booking process for Egyptian travelers?
  2. How might we balance clear pricing and upselling without confusing users?
  3. How might we simplify seat selection and make flight choices more flexible?

Crazy 8s

Brainstormed layout ideas using Crazy 8s to determine the most intuitive booking steps and navigation. Created a clear Information Architecture, ensuring users can navigate from flight selection to payment with minimal steps and easy access to essential details.

04.
PROTOTYPE

Creating an intuitive user experience

Wireframes & User Flow

Developed wireframes to outline the essential booking steps, from homepage to payment confirmation. The user flow prioritized ease, with a clear path for selecting flights, seats, and flexible date options.

Low Fidelity Prototype

Tested the initial layout with users to gather feedback on navigation and clarity. Users appreciated the streamlined design but suggested clearer labels on flexible date options.

High Fidelity Prototype

Incorporated visual refinements, interactive seat selection, and simplified payment screens. The design system ensured cohesive typography, color schemes, and UI components aligned with Misr Fly’s brand.

Design System

Established a design system to standardize elements, including colors, fonts, and icons, creating a consistent visual experience across the platform.

Usability Testing

Tested usability with six users to find areas for improvement. Users liked payment flow but wanted clearer confirmation indicators, like final review page or "Payment Confirmed" message. Added cues for better user confidence and smoother booking.

05.
REFLECTION

Improving the app based on user feedback

Key Insights

  • Users appreciated the ability to filter direct flights and select seats seamlessly within the booking flow.
  • Clear, transparent pricing options increased user satisfaction with the platform.
  • A distraction-free design created a better overall experience by keeping the focus on flight booking essentials.

Final Revisions

Based on feedback, I made several key revisions:

  • Enhanced seat selection with clear labels and a pop-up selection interface.
  • Streamlined the booking panel to focus on clarity, minimizing redundant elements.
  • Focused on maintaining an intuitive, easy-to-navigate interface that offers flexible booking options without unnecessary steps.

What I Learned

This project Emphasized the importance of providing Egyptian travelers with a responsive, intuitive design that feels familiar and accessible. Incorporating feedback allowed me to refine the booking experience, ensuring Misr Fly offers a convenient, user-friendly interface aligned with local expectations.

Next Steps

Continued usability testing will help optimize the user experience further, focusing on refining booking flow and integrating additional travel features, such as loyalty rewards and Premium seating options.
