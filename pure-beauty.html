Skincare Made Simple

Brief

Pure Beauty is a mobile app designed to revolutionize the skincare shopping experience in Egypt. By offering personalized product recommendations and routine management , the app empowers users to make informed decisions, ensuring that skincare shopping is simple , enjoyable , and effective .

Starting thoughts…

Pure Beauty is a mobile app designed to revolutionize the skincare shopping experience in Egypt. By offering personalized product recommendations and routine management , the app empowers users to make informed decisions, ensuring that skincare shopping is simple , enjoyable , and effective .

Solution

The Glow-tine Routine Manager

Pure Beauty simplifies the skincare shopping experience with personalized product recommendations , interactive features , and routine management tools , all tailored to meet the unique needs of Egyptian consumers. The app’s standout feature, the Glow-tine Routine Manager , empowers users to effortlessly integrate new products into their skincare routines and track their progress.

Enjoy the skincare journey

During my research, I found that offering a wide range of skincare products without guidance can be overwhelming and ineffective for consumers. To truly enhance the skincare experience, it’s essential to provide personalized routines and product recommendations. By tailoring product suggestions to individual skin types and concerns, Pure Beauty helps users stay motivated and engaged in their skincare journey.

Curate Your Routine

01.
DISCOVER

What drives skincare shopping complexity for Egyptian consumers?

Secondary Research

The global skincare market is thriving, with Egypt’s growth being equally promising. Yet, the overwhelming number of skincare options makes it challenging for consumers to find the right products. Personalized skincare brands are emerging but offer limited selections. Large beauty retailers provide variety, but lack meaningful guidance.

Screener Survey

After understanding the global and Egyptian market trends, I conducted a screener survey to gauge how Egyptian consumers interact with skincare products. The survey revealed that most users feel confused by the sheer number of options and seek personalized guidance.

User Interview

From the survey, I selected several participants for deeper interviews. These users often turned to large retailers but felt unsatisfied with the guidance offered. They expressed a strong desire for an app that simplifies product selection based on their specific skincare needs.

Expert Interview

To validate the user pain points and needs, I interviewed skincare experts who emphasized the importance of providing users with accurate, tailored product recommendations and a holistic skincare journey.

Expert Insight

  • Misconception of Users: Many users don't understand how to combine active ingredients, leading to ineffective or even harmful routines.

  • Professional Guidance: Estheticians recommend creating tailored routines that balance hydration, treatment, and protection.

  • Improvement Suggestion: The expert suggested that the Pure Beauty app could offer virtual consultations or AI-powered product suggestions to help users navigate complex ingredient interactions.

Competitor Analysis

I began by analyzing both direct and indirect competitors in the skincare industry, focusing on those offering personalized routines, product recommendations, and shopping experiences. This helped me identify key opportunities to enhance Pure Beauty’s user experience by integrating features that address gaps in guidance, routine management, and product selection.

03.
IDEATE

How can we simplify the skincare shopping experience for Egyptian consumers?

Problem Statements

After analyzing Sara’s journey, I focused on three main challenges:

  1. How might we make product selection less confusing?
  2. How might we help users create skincare routines tailored to their needs?
  3. How might we provide users with the knowledge to make informed skincare decisions?

Crazy 8s

I used the Crazy 8s brainstorming exercise to generate initial ideas for the app’s key features. After several rounds, I honed in on the concept of an evolving, personalized Routine Builder that would help users manage their skincare routines while receiving curated product recommendations.

04.
PROTOTYPE

Creating an intuitive user experience

Wireframes & User Flow

Informed by my ideation, I designed the Glow-tine Routine Manager and built wireframes around this central feature. The Glow-tine feature allows users to organize their skincare products into routines, shop for new items, and receive personalized tips. I ensured that the user flow was seamless from the moment they opened the app to the point of finalizing a purchase.

Low Fidelity Prototype

I created several low-fidelity prototypes to visualize core user flows and gather initial feedback. This process helped refine the design structure and prioritize essential features before transitioning to high-fidelity versions.

High Fidelity Prototype

I created high-fidelity prototypes to explore design variations, enabling user testing and feedback to refine the experience.

Sticker Sheet

A sticker sheet was developed to ensure visual consistency across the app. This included color schemes, typography, and reusable components, ensuring that each element aligned with the brand and user expectations.

Usability Testing

During the low-fidelity prototype testing, participants valued the personalized product recommendations but felt the shopping experience should come first, with educational content as a secondary focus. When testing the high-fidelity prototype, users appreciated the introduction of the Glow-tine Routine Manager for organizing routines. They reiterated the need to prioritize shopping, while finding the ingredient highlights useful for making confident product decisions.

05.
REFLECTION

Improving the app based on user feedback

Key Insights

  • Users wanted more focus on shopping first, with educational content accessible as a secondary option.
  • The Glow-tine Routine Manager was well-received for its ability to help users manage and integrate new products into their routine.
  • Ingredient breakdowns were appreciated but needed to be presented more clearly in the shopping process.

Final Revisions

Based on feedback, I made several key revisions:

  • Enhanced the visual hierarchy of the shopping flow to prioritize product recommendations.
  • Simplified the design of the Routine Builder and reduced clutter on the Glow-tine screen.
  • Made the educational content more accessible via clickable banners rather than overwhelming users with text.

What I Learned

Building Pure Beauty taught me the importance of balancing a user-friendly interface with personalized features. By listening to user feedback, I was able to refine the app and make it a truly transformative experience for Egyptian skincare consumers.

Next Steps

In the next phase, I will continue testing and refining the Glow-tine Routine Builder to ensure it delivers the best possible experience for users. The goal is to launch a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) that can be expanded with additional features like AI-powered product recommendations and routine reminders.
